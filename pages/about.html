Who we are

More than a
restaurant.

Nestled in the heart of Waterford Lakes, Jacob's Café was born from a simple passion: to create a space where food brings people together. We blend Italian gelato tradition with bold Mediterranean-inspired signature dishes, Turkish breakfast classics, and a specialty coffee program that makes every morning worth waking up for.

Our recently renovated space carries a warm European aesthetic — every detail was chosen to make you feel welcome, whether you're stopping in for a solo espresso, a weekend brunch with family, or a long evening meal with friends.

As a locally owned business, we're deeply connected to the Orlando community. When you dine with us, you're supporting a family — and we never forget that.

What drives us

Our values

Fresh ingredients

We source fresh, quality ingredients and prepare everything in-house — from our house-made gelato to our sauces and seasonings. If it doesn't meet our standard, it doesn't reach your table.

Genuine warmth

Every guest is treated like family. Our staff is trained to be attentive, knowledgeable, and genuinely happy to have you here. The food is great — the people make it exceptional.

Community roots

We're not a chain. We're your neighbors. Jacob's Cafe is locally owned and deeply connected to the Waterford Lakes and UCF community — your support keeps us here and keeps us growing.
